# Agasobanuye x Oshakur Script Format This repository uses a dual-narrative structural format. It combines the raw, philosophical poetry of **Oshakur** with the high-energy, real-time voice-over commentary of **Agasobanuye**. --- ## 📋 The Structure ### 1. The Setup (Intro) * `[Oshakur Quote]` -> A short, deep quote setting the emotional mood. * `[Agasobanuye Intro]` -> The commentator breaks the wall, introduces the character, and hypes the drama. ### 2. The Verse & Voice-Over (The Core) * `[Oshakur Bars]` -> Active-voice lines dealing with survival, pain, or hope. * `[Agasobanuye Translation]` -> High-energy summary translating the deep meaning into street-smart warnings. ### 3. The Climax (The Conflict) * `[Oshakur Heat]` -> A tense, dramatic lyrical moment. * `[Agasobanuye Breakdown]` -> Beat-by-beat explanation of the action so the audience gets the lesson. ### 4. The Outro (The Lesson) * `[Oshakur Final Word]` -> A philosophical sign-off about society or growth. * `[Agasobanuye Fade Out]` -> Commentator closes the show, leaving the audience hyped. --- ## 📝 Script Template (Copy & Paste) ```text =================================================================== [INTRO] =================================================================== OSHAKUR: > "Insert deep, poetic lyric about the struggle here." AGASOBANUYE: > "Yego kabisa! Look at our brother now. Watch closely, the story starts here!" =================================================================== [BODY VERSE] =================================================================== OSHAKUR: > "Insert short lines about survival, friends, or betrayal here." AGASOBANUYE: > "Urakoze! Did you hear that? He says watch out for fake people! Look at that guy!" =================================================================== [THE CLIMAX] =================================================================== OSHAKUR: > "Insert the most intense, dramatic lines here." AGASOBANUYE: > "Ayiee! It is going down! He has to make a choice right now!" =================================================================== [OUTRO] =================================================================== OSHAKUR: > "Insert a final philosophical thought about tomorrow here." AGASOBANUYE: > "And that is how it ends. Keep your head up. Seamless cut, peace!" =================================================================== ```
